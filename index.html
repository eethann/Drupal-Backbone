Drupal Backbone Resources

Welcome! Thank you for your interest in our module! We would like to offer the following resource to make your experience an pleasurable and fruitful one.

Drupal Backbone Module Resources

  • First, check out the Introducing Backbone for Drupal post to get the lay of the land and the slides from the Backbone BoF at DrupalCon Denver.
  • You'll probably want to go from there into the JS annotated source for the base drupalbackbone.js and the services-specific extensions.
  • Finally, to get a sense what a Backbone App module looks like, check out the example application source code.

Backbone Resources

There are a ton of great resources on Backbone. Here are a few of our top recommendations:

  • The Backbone Peepcode screencast
  • The Backbone Fundamentals open source e-book
  • The Backbone site
  • The Backbone annotated source code
  • Check out the Backbone.js resource list on quora.com for an extensive collection.
